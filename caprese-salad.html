Caprese Salad

Caprese salad is a classic Italian dish that never fails to impress with its simplicity and fresh flavors. Made with juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil leaves, it is the perfect appetizer or side dish for any occasion. The salad's colorful presentation makes it a beautiful addition to any table, and its light and refreshing taste make it a perfect complement to a variety of other dishes. Pair it with grilled chicken, seafood, or pasta, and you'll have a complete and satisfying meal that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Enjoy the fresh taste of summer with this delicious Caprese salad recipe!

Course: Side
Prep: 10 mins
Cooking: None
Difficulty: Easy

Servings:

4

Ingredients

  • 200g heritage tomatoes
  • 1 large shallot, sliced thinly
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 drops of Tabasco
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 6 basil leaves shredded
  • 150g buffalo mozzarella or burrata

Method

Step 1

Put the tomatoes in a bowl with the shallots and season with a large pinch of salt. Leave for 10 minutes for the tomatoes to release their juices.

Step 2

Lift the tomatoes and shallots out of the bowl using a slotted spoon, and reserve the juices. Arrange the tomatoes over two plates, then sprinkle over the shallots.

Step 3

Whisk the olive oil, Tabasco and vinegar into the reserved tomato juices to make a dressing. Drizzle over the tomatoes and shallots, then scatter with the shredded basil. Put one half of the mozzarella in the centre of each plate, and season with freshly ground black pepper. Top with the extra basil leaves, and serve.
